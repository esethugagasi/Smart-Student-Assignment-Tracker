Smart Student Assignment Tracker Reflection

Challenges
Balancing stakeholder needs was a key challenge. Students require a simple and fast system, while IT staff prioritize security and performance. Increasing system features can negatively affect performance, requiring trade-offs.

Another challenge was writing requirements that are specific and measurable rather than vague.

Trade-offs
Simplicity vs functionality: limited features to core requirements
Performance vs notifications: reduced frequency of reminders
Security vs usability: added authentication without complicating login

Lessons Learned
Requirements must be measurable and testable
Stakeholder needs often conflict and must be balanced
Clear documentation improves system design and communication

Conclusion
This assignment strengthened understanding of requirement engineering and the importance of traceability. It highlighted how structured requirements directly influence system architecture and development success.
